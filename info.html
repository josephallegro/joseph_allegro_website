Joseph here. I’m a graphic designer and writer based in Rhode Island.

What I design
Brand identities, mostly. Also websites, animations, signage, and typefaces.

What I write
Most of the writing I do happens as part of the design process. Outside of the studio, I write fiction for fun.

Currently, I’m working at Minelli. I’m also taking on projects with Merl, a design collective I founded with four friends. In 2018, I received a BFA in Graphic Design with a minor in Literary Arts and Studies from RISD.
