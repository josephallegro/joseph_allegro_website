FIM

As a designer at Minelli, I worked on the colorful brand identity for FIM (Flint Institute of Music), a large, multifaceted performing arts organization based in Flint, Michigan. We developed a robust brand toolkit and architecture that has significantly raised the community’s awareness of FIM and all they do.
