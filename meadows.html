Meadows

I designed this typeface at RISD under the instruction of type designer Richard Lipton. The letterforms are friendly with an edge, inspired by the whimsical yet slightly sinister tone found in Scandinavian folklore. In October 2017, Meadows was awarded 2nd prize in the Updike Student Type Design Competition.
